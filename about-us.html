Our Story

Empowering Dreams
Through Education

Founded in 2023, Parwaaz Scholars is a mission-driven non-profit dedicated to unlocking the potential of exceptional students. "Parwaaz" means flight—symbolizing our commitment to helping students soar above financial barriers.

Our Mission

To identify and nurture exceptional students by providing comprehensive scholarships and mentorship that enables them to become leaders who impact their communities.

Our Vision

A world where every deserving student has access to quality higher education, serving as a catalyst for positive social change and global development.

Core Values

Our Guiding Principles

The values that define our mission and guide every student's flight path.

Compassion

Supporting every student's unique journey with empathy and understanding.

Integrity

Operating with transparency and the highest ethical standards in all we do.

Innovation

Constantly evolving our methods to better serve students in a changing world.

Inclusivity

Ensuring opportunities are accessible to all, regardless of background.

Our History

The Parwaaz Journey

From a small community group to a formal scholarship foundation.

2023 Founding

The Inception

Started as "PakHazara", a grassroots initiative via a WhatsApp group to support local talent in the Hazara region.

2024 Growth

Digital Expansion

Launched our digital presence and began building a global network of mentors to guide students through university applications.

2025 Present

The Flight

Formalizing as Parwaaz Scholars. Scaling our impact through institutional partnerships and a full-scale scholarship portal.

500+ Students Supported
15+ Workshops Held
20+ Partner Institutions

Empowering exceptional students to soar to new heights through comprehensive educational scholarships, mentorship, and opportunities.
