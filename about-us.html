About Parwaaz Scholars

Empowering Dreams Through Education

Founded in 2015, Parwaaz Scholars is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing educational opportunities to exceptional students regardless of their financial background. "Parwaaz" means "flight" in Urdu, symbolizing our mission to help students soar to new heights.

Our Mission

To identify, support, and nurture exceptional students by providing comprehensive educational scholarships, mentorship, and opportunities that enable them to achieve their academic goals and become leaders who positively impact their communities.

Our Vision

A world where every deserving student has access to quality higher education, regardless of their socioeconomic background, and where education serves as a catalyst for positive social change and global development.
